Habitat:

Lives in the void between galaxies, this habitat is characterized by extreme cold and high radiation levels.
The Void Wanderer has adapted to survive in this harsh environment by developing a tough exoskeleton and the ability to feed on cosmic dust and energy.
It is often found near black holes, where it can use the intense gravitational forces to its advantage, allowing it to collect its losten body parts.

Appearance:

It is a small circular body filled with black energy. It had lost all of its body parts during the failed experiemnt in the lab.
Through the portals, it collects its lost body parts, which are scattered across the universe. It has a pair of glowing eyes that can see in the dark, and it emits a faint hum as it moves through space.

Key Characteristics:

  • Travels through the void.
  • Feeds on cosmic dust and energy.
  • Feels lost and alone.
