Void Wanderer

Scientific Name:

Errantis vacuus

Discovered by / at:

One of the grad students in Professor Snider's lab

Family:

Portalidaes

Habitat:

Exists between dimensions in empty voids.
It opens circular portals to move from one location to another.
It searches for its scattered body parts near dimensional rifts.

Appearance:

A small white circular body with a dark inner eye. It lost its body parts during a failed experiment.
It grows rotating external spikes as it collects each part.
When clicked, it glitches into overlapping erratic circles.

Key Characteristics:

  • Opens spinning circular portals to travel between voids.
  • Searches for its five lost body parts.
  • Flees when the mouse gets close.
  • Was never supposed to exist.
  • Glitches into erratic patterns when clicked.

What we know so far about the Void Wanderer? The Void Wanderer is an accidental result of a failed experiment. It wanders between dimensions, opening spinning portals to search for its five lost body parts.
It is insecure and avoids being observed. Once all parts are recovered, it keeps teleporting between portals in a loop.

Guidelines for human interaction with this creature Move your mouse close to it and it will flee.
Click and hold on it to trigger its glitch state.
Be patient and watch it collect its parts and travel through portals.
